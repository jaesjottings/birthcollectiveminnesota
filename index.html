Welcome to The Birth Collective

Building community around pregnancy, birth, and postpartum

For Everyone

The Birth Collective's goal is to help connect parents and birth workers to what they need during pregnancy, birth, and postpartum—whether that's education, support, community, or connection with birth workers. We're revealing our community's "best-kept secrets" and making life easier for families.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 For Parents & Families

Whether you're expecting your first baby or growing your family, we're here to support you.

  • Find trusted, local birth workers
  • Access education and resources
  • Connect with other expectant and new parents
  • Learn what to expect at each stage of pregnancy
  • Build your support village

💼 For Birth Workers

Connect with families who need your expertise and build relationships with fellow birth workers.

  • Be visible to families in our community
  • Network with other birth professionals
  • Contribute your knowledge and experience
  • Help shape the future of birth support
  • Connect with families seeking your services

About The Birth Collective

Our story and vision for community

What We Are

The Birth Collective Alexandria is two-fold:

📱 Online Community

An online gathering place to build community, ask questions, and connect around pregnancy, birth, and postpartum topics. We value support, connection, empowerment, education, and advocacy. Our dream is to help new families create their village and make it simple to find answers about pregnancy, birth, postpartum, and beyond.

🤝 Quarterly Gatherings

We host quarterly meet-ups in Alexandria, Minnesota to connect in person, share birth stories, network with regional birth workers, and explore deeper topics together around pregnancy, birth, and postpartum. These gatherings are where our community comes alive—where families find their people and birth workers strengthen their networks.

Our Community, Our Energy

We are energized by the response to our first gathering. At that meeting, we shared our personal journeys and heard powerful stories from families and birth workers. We explored what our community truly needs and identified important gaps in support.

We're committed to building something meaningful together. If you're interested in being part of this conversation—as a parent, birth worker, or community member—we'd love to hear from you.

Meet the Founders

Allie Drexler

Prenatal Educator, Registered Nurse

Allie is passionate about creating spaces where families feel seen, heard, and supported. With years of experience in maternal health, she brings both clinical knowledge and genuine care to everything she does. Allie believes every family deserves access to compassionate, informative support—and that our community is strongest when we work together.

Jamie Shelley

APRN, CNM, IBCLC

Jamie is a certified nurse-midwife and lactation consultant with a deep commitment to whole-person and family-centered care. She works at Empowered Midwifery providing midwifery services rooted in shared decision-making and evidence-based practice. She's convinced that life is better lived in community than in isolation, which is why she co-founded The Birth Collective to build a space where families have access to knowledgeable, caring professionals and each other.
